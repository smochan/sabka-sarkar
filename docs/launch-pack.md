# Apni Sarkar — Launch & Social Pack

Everything you need for Thursday's CJP protest in Pune + the social launch.
Tone: hopeful, defiant, non-partisan. Lead with the idea, never attack a person.

---

## The 20-second pitch (for the CJP founder)

> "CJP gave 22 million people somewhere to be angry. Apni Sarkar gives them something to build. It's a live site where the public nominates the India they actually want — the best doctor as health minister, the best teacher as education minister — each backed by a real council of 50 people from that field, with a 5-year plan. No politicians, no appointments, no one owns it. It's open-source and it runs itself. You point the movement at it; the movement fills it in."

Why it helps CJP: it converts outrage → a concrete, positive, shareable artifact, and every nomination is a signup. It's a tool for the movement, not a rival to it.

## Launch reel concept (15-20s, vertical)

- 0-3s: Text on black — "They called the youth cockroaches."
- 3-6s: Hard cut to paper-white — "So we wrote our own government."
- 6-12s: Fast scroll through the cabinet cards (screen recording of the live site), portfolio names popping: Education. Health. Jobs. Climate. Justice.
- 12-16s: A finger taps "upvote", the count ticks up.
- 16-20s: Wordmark अपनी सरकार + "Nominate your cabinet. Link in bio."
- Audio: building tabla/electronic track. **Never render the key text with AI — overlay it in the editor.**
